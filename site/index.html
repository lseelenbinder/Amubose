amubosé

Ever needed to search Amazon for a whole list of books? Yeah. So have I, and it is awful.

Up until now you had to search

one. stinkin'. book. at. a. time.

Amubosé fixes that. how?

  1. Enter your list of books separated by semi-colons, pipes (|), or one per line.
  2. Have some ISBNs? Those will go straight to the product page.
  3. Press "Get 'em."
  4. Enjoy.
